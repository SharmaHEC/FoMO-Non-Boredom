Virtual Horse Race

In this game, you will take part in a virtual horse race with four horses. Your role is to select one horse and then watch the race unfold.

Please follow these steps:

  1. Choose one horse to back in the race.
  2. After you confirm your choice, the race will start automatically.
  3. Once the race begins, you cannot change your chosen horse.

During the race you will see a button labeled “Cheer your horse”. Each press spends some of your limited cheer energy to give your horse a short boost. Your cheer energy bar refills gradually between presses, which limits how often you can cheer. Remember, other players also have a similar option.

In addition, if you win this race, you would qualify to take part in a lottery in which the winner receives an iPad.

Choose your horse

Click on a horse to select it, then click on "Confirm Choice" to start the race.
